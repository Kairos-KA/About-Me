Milgram is an interactive music project that was created by DECO*27 and Yamanaka Takuya. The story follows Es, an amnesiac who is tasked to judge ten prisoners as "Forgiven" or "Not-Forgiven". These prisoners have all committed murder and we are told to "not be fooled by their appearances " and that "they are all murderers who can't be trusted" by Jackalope, a talking rabbit.

Es, who is the warden, must listen to each prisoner's extracted song and determine what crime the prisoner has committed. Each song contains hints regarding their crimes.

There are three trials, each trial will reveal more about the prisoner's crime. The direction the characters song goes will be dependent on their verdict.
